Two Powerful Lab Tools

Built for researchers, refined for simplicity

Core Tool

PlateMaster Pro

Design multi-well plate layouts with color-coded treatment groups, calculate CFU concentrations, plan dilution series, and export publication-ready plate maps in multiple formats.

  • 6 / 12 / 24 / 48 / 96 / 384-well formats
  • Single & multi-strain CFU calculator
  • Serial dilution generator (1:10 → 1:10⁶)
  • Colony counter with dilution correction
  • Export PNG, CSV & TXT protocol
  • Universal dilution (C₁V₁ = C₂V₂)
Launch PlateMaster Pro
Specialized

Hydrogel Calculator

Precision formulation calculator for dual-syringe hydrogel systems. Optimized for GelMA + Fibrinogen/Thrombin bioinks used in tissue engineering and 3D bioprinting applications.

  • Dual-syringe (A + B) formulation
  • GelMA concentration targeting (% w/v)
  • Fibrinogen + Thrombin volumes
  • Cell suspension integration
  • Real-time recalculation on input
  • CaCl₂ / buffer top-up outputs
